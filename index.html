FAQs: Everything You Need to Know
About Nelieca

Explore the answers to common queries. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, our support team is always ready to assist you.

How does Nelieca ensure quality in educational content? +
Nelieca partners with top educators and curates every course through peer review, continuous feedback, and content standardization.
Can I use Nelieca for live classes and recordings? +
Yes, Nelieca supports both live teaching and recording playback with classroom tools like whiteboards, quizzes, and engagement tracking.
Is there support for educators new to online teaching? +
Absolutely! We offer onboarding help, tutorials, and 24/7 support so educators can get comfortable with the platform quickly.
